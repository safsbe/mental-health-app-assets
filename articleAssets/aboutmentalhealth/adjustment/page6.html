IMPACT ON NS

1. Emotional Challenges
Feelings of sadness, anxiety, or being overwhelmed can make it hard to focus, complete tasks, or interact with others.

2. Behavioral Changes
Irritability or withdrawal from peers can affect relationships and teamwork, making communication and collaboration more difficult.

3. Impact on Work Performance
Difficulty concentrating or low motivation can lead to challenges with training, duties, and responsibilities.

4. Physical Symptoms
Headaches, body aches, poor sleep, and changes in appetite can drain your energy, making it harder to cope with NS demands.

5. Increased Stress from Personal Issues
Personal struggles, such as family problems, can add extra stress and make adjusting to NS even harder.
