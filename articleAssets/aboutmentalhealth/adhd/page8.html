TREATMENT

At present, no treatments have been found to cure this disorder, but many treatments exist to effectively manage and ease the symptoms such as medication and therapy.

Medication can come with side effects that differ among individuals, especially on first intake, but should reduce over time e.g.,

  • Insomnia or difficulty sleeping
  • Loss of appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Nervousness or irritability
  • Mood swings
  • Headaches and stomachaches
