SYMPTOMS

OCD can often be spotted by watching a person's repetitive behaviors.

Common signs include:

  • Cleanliness: Fear contamination, leading them to wash their hands or clean their home repeatedly.

  • Order: Fixated on symmetry and order and might spend a lot of time rearranging items like books, cutlery, or cushions to relieve anxiety.

  • Hoarding: Struggles to throw things away, collecting old newspapers, clothes, and other items without any reason.

  • Counting: Repeatedly counting things, like the number of steps they take or the lights in a hallway. If they lose count, they start over.

  • Safety: Irrational fears about safety and check things like doors, windows, or appliances repeatedly.
