TYPES OF OCD
(Obsessions VS Compulsions)

Common obsessions include:

  • Fear of contamination
  • Doubts about things (e.g., locking doors, turning off stoves)
  • Needing things to be symmetrical or in order
  • Hoarding
  • Religious concerns
  • Aggression or violent thoughts
  • Sexual thoughts

Examples of OCD obsessions:

  • Fear of germs from shaking hands or touching things others have touched
  • Doubts about whether the door is locked or the stove is off
  • Repeated thoughts of causing harm in a traffic accident
  • Feeling distressed if objects aren't arranged in the right way
  • Imagining hurting someone
    or acting inappropriately
