IMPACT ON NS

Those with unmanageable depressive episodes can be very aware of their surrounding and the things that they are doing, at times with impaired decision-making ability to take the right and safe approach. This poses risks not only to self but others around them, especially in high-risk activities.

Being socially withdrawn from people around them meant they may lack social support to help them through the tough times. They may also be seen as not a team player, in turn being shun.

Nevertheless, serviceman with depression can still serve NS with the appropriate adjustment at work and compliance to their treatment plan to effectively manage their symptoms. For instance, helping them establish a routine or find an enjoyable hobby may induce a sense of purpose.
