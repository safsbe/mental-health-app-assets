SYMPTOMS

Individuals with anxiety disorder often feel excessive fear about specific situations or everyday situations.

These symptoms usually last for several months, and people tend to avoid the situations that make them anxious.

Other symptoms of anxiety disorders can include:

  • Trouble concentrating or making decisions
  • Feeling irritable, tense, or restless
  • Nausea or stomach discomfort
  • Sweating, trembling, or shaking
  • A sense of impending danger, panic, or doom
  • Heart palpitations
  • Trouble sleeping
