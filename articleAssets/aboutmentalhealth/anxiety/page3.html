Genes can play a role in anxiety disorders, but it is also linked to other mental health conditions, physical health, and excessive use of substances.

Biological Causes

  • Chemical imbalances in the body
  • Environmental influences

For example, a child who grew up under a strict father may feel similar stress when working with a supervisor who behaves the same way.

Excessive use of substances
For example,

  • Caffeine
  • Alcohol, or
  • Tobacco

Other mental health conditions
Anyone can develop an anxiety disorder, but those who have experienced abuse, severe losses, or other difficult situations are more likely to.
