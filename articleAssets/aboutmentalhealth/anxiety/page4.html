TYPES OF ANXIETY DISORDERS

There are different types of anxiety disorders, including:

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD): Excessive worry about daily events.
  • Panic Disorder: Panic attacks and fear of future attacks.
  • Social Anxiety Disorder: Fear of social situations and being judged.
  • Agoraphobia: Fear of situations where escape may be difficult.
  • Separation Anxiety Disorder: Fear of being separated from loved ones.
  • Specific Phobias: Intense fear of specific objects or situations.
  • Selective Mutism: Inability to speak in certain social settings, mostly in children.

People can have more than one anxiety disorder, and symptoms often start in childhood or adolescence.
