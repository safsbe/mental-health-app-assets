Everyone feels anxious sometimes, but when does it become a health concern?

Anxiety is the feeling of worry or nervousness, like before an exam or presentation. This 'good anxiety' can motivate you to prepare, and once the situation is over, the worry fades away.

It is a normal reaction to stress, helping you stay alert to potential dangers. However, if anxiety leads to physical symptoms like a fast heartbeat, dizziness, or breathlessness, and affects your ability to function, it could be the start of an anxiety disorder.
