THE HIDDEN CONSEQUENCES

Burnout isn’t just about feeling drained; It can have serious effects on both mental and physical health.

Studies have linked burnout to various issues such as:

  • Physical and Mental Health concerns – Increased risk of heart disease, high blood pressure, sleep disturbances, anxiety, and depression.

  • Unhealthy Coping Mechanisms – Higher chances of relying on alcohol, smoking, or other substances.

  • Strained Relationships – Feeling emotionally distant, irritable, or disconnected from peers, superiors, and loved ones.

  • Impact on performance – Reduced performance, lack of engagement, and a sense of helplessness about future goals.
