TREATMENT

✅ Acknowledge Your Feelings – Recognizing that you’re struggling is the first step toward finding solutions.

✅ Prioritize Rest and Recovery – Ensure proper sleep, nutrition, and breaks to maintain energy levels.

✅ Communicate with Your Superiors – If work demands are excessive, discuss possible adjustments.

✅ Seek Support – Talk to peers, trusted commanders, or professionals for guidance and assistance.

✅ Engage in Stress-Relief Activities – Exercise, hobbies, and mindfulness techniques can help restore balance.
