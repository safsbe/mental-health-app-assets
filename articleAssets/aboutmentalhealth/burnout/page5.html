IMPACT ON NS

The 5 Stages of Burnout in NS:

Burnout doesn’t happen all at once—it develops gradually through five stages. Recognizing these signs early can help prevent long-term struggles.

Stage 1: The Honeymoon Phase
At the start of NS, you may feel motivated, committed, and eager to take on new tasks. There’s a sense of excitement as you adapt to military life, build camaraderie, and tackle challenges head-on.

Stage 2: Onset of Stress
As responsibilities increase, stress starts creeping in. You may notice signs like fatigue, reduced productivity, difficulty concentrating, and disrupted sleep. The initial enthusiasm may begin to wear off.
