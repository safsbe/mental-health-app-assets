Which type of therapy should it be?

This depends on the needs and desired outcome. Far beyond the media portrayal of a person lying on a couch during therapy, therapy session can be individual, couple, family or even in group settings. There are also many techniques and approaches a mental health practitioner can utilise. Each comes with its pros and cons.

Studies have found that a person took an average of 2.5 therapists to find a good match. So be patient.

How long is a therapy session?

It can last about 40-120 mins, and may take place once a month or a few times a month as discussed between us and our therapists.
