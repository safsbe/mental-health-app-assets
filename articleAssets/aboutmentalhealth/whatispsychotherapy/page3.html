When do we need therapy?

We may benefit from having therapy if
  • The sense of helplessness is overwhelming and for a prolonged period
  • Nothing we have tried till date seems to work
  • It is affecting our daily activities, and we cease to function
  • Our action or words are hurting ourselves or others
  • We want to develop specific personal skills

Is therapy for life?

No, it really depends on the severity of the problem and progress made during the session. Remember, therapy is always collaborative, so be an active participant and work with your therapist.
