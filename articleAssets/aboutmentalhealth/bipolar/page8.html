TREATMENT

Medicine is an essential part of the treatment plan but depending on the severity and characteristics shown by the individual, a mixture of psychotherapy or psychosocial intervention can be implemented to balance the side-effects, functioning needs, and effectiveness.

If you have or believe you have Bipolar Disorder and it is severely impacting your life, do seek professional help as soon as you can! :) Remember, with the right treatment, these problems can be managed to help you go through your NS life smoothly and improve your overall mental well-being/ functioning in the future!
