Bipolar disorder (formerly called manic-depressive illness or manic depression) can cause unusual swings in a person's mood, energy level, and concentration. Transiting through the various period of high energy (maniac episodes) and low energy (depressive episodes) can be disruptive to their well-being, in turn affect their day-to-day activities as well as their relationship with others.

While there is no direct cure for bipolar, the symptoms can be effectively managed through long term and continuous treatment such as combination of medication and psychotherapy.
