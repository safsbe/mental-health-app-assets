SYMPTOMS

Maniac episode

  • More agitation, irritation, or "touchy"

  • Engage in risky and impulsive behaviours, such as spending a lot of money or reckless driving
  • Make grand and unrealistic plans
  • Show poor judgment and decision making

  • Feel very “high,” or elated
  • Talk really fast about a lot of different things
  • Have a lot of energy and increased activity level
