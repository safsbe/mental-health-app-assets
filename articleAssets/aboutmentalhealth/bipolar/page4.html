Bipolar 1 Disorder Short manic episodes, or severe manic symptoms that needs immediate hospital care. Depressive episodes usually occur for a period of time as well.
Bipolar II Disorder Defined by a pattern of depressive episodes and hypomanic episodes, but without the full-blown manic symptoms.
Cyclothymic Disorder (also called Cyclothymia) Defined by numerous periods of hypomanic symptoms and depressive symptoms lasting for a period of time. However, the symptoms do not meet the diagnostic requirements for a hypomanic episode and a depressive episode to be classified as above.
Others Defined by Bipolar Disorder symptoms that do not match the three categories listed above
