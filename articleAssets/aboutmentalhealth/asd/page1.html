Date of post: 14 Apr 24

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a type of lifelong neurodevelopmental condition that can be detected in early childhood. Based on World Health Organisation, about 1 in 100 children has autism. Characteristics differ across individual but generally causes key difficulties in social interaction and communication and can exhibit restrictive or repetitive behaviours/ interest that can cause some dysfunction in school, work or other aspects of life. Early and proper treatment can help people with ASD build the relevant social skills to lead a more positive life!
