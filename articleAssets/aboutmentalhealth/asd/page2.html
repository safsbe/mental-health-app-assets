WHAT IS IT

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition that typically appear in the early part of your life and last through your lifetime. Being a spectrum type of disorder, there is a wide range in the type and severity of symptoms. People with ASD are known to have more challenges with social interactions, communications and also exhibit restrictive or repetitive behaviours/ interests, which can cause some dysfunction in their school, work or other aspects of life.
