WHAT IS IT

Stress is your body’s natural reaction to any demand that disrupts your usual routine and it is unavoidable. Too much stress due to intensive or prolonged worries about things like money, jobs, relationships, or health can be harmful (distress).

However, stress can also be useful (eustress) when you learn to manage it over time with more abilities! Eustress typically:

  • Are of short intervals
  • Can motivates us or make the task exciting overall
  • Can focus our energy
  • Can improves our performance
  • Something within our coping ability (that is why we need to always expand our ability!)
