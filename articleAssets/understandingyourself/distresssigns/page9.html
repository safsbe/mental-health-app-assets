Still unsure what is your stress level?

Try this Perceived Stress Scale survey to get a sensing (PSS-14):

https://www.psytoolkit.org/cgi-bin/3.4.4/survey?s=hWZu8

However, do note that this is not a diagnostic tool. It serves as a guide to highlight if you are having high perceived stress (> 27).

Reach out to a professional if you are lost about what to do!
