Personality test can be a quick way to gain awareness of yourself. Taking several personality tests allows for a more comprehensive assessment and understanding of why you think, feel or behave in certain ways, in turn identify your strengths and weaknesses. But it is not an exhaustive or permanent description of how you are wired or who you can be! This is only the first step to help you work towards personal development, career planning, team building, or understanding your interpersonal dynamics!
