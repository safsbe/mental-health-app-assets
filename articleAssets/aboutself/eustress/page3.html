Eustress

Negative stress response characterized by negative emotions and physiological reactions

Arises when one is overwhelmed by demands, losses, or perceived threats

Harmful as it can lead to serious health issues like high blood pressure, heart disease, obesity, diabetes and even suicide

Positive stress response that involves optimal levels of stimulation

Occurs in response to challenging but manageable and rewarding tasks e.g., healthy competition

Beneficial as it generates a sense of accomplishment, facilitates personal growth and development, and supports high performance
