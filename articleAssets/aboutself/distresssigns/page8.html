Step 2:

Use the following to help you zoom into the specific stress and identify possible action to reduce it. Keep a journal to track recurring patterns or express out the negativity.

  • List the challenges that causes distress
  • Select one example from the list above
  • How did this distress impact you physically, emotionally and/or cognitively
  • What is stopping you from doing it in a more helpful manner
  • What would happen if it cannot be resolved
  • How can the stress be broken down into manageable parts
  • What can I do now that is within my control to reduce the stress
  • What are things that I am grateful for despite the stress
