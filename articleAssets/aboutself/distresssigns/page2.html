WHAT IS IT?

Stress is your body’s natural reaction to any demand that disrupts your usual routine. A little bit of stress (eustress) can be useful, like when it helps you overcome a fear or gives you extra energy to finish a task.

However, there’s also harmful stress (distress), which comes from intensive or prolonged worries about things like money, jobs, relationships, or health. Just like a “check engine” light in your car, if you ignore these signals, you might face bigger problems in the future.
