HOW TO APPLY IT

What can we do if our sleep is constantly disrupted?

First, it is important to identify the unhelpful factors that are disrupting your sleep such as:

  • Your lifestyle e.g., diet/ caffeine intake/ activity level
  • Stress level
  • Exposure to light e.g., from electronic devices
  • Sleep disorder e.g., sleep apnea or restless legs syndrome
  • Noise or disturbances within your sleep environment

Keeping a sleep diary is one way to track the factors that help/hinder sleep.

Second, make relevant changes so you can commit to a consistent sleep schedule to allow your body to cycle through the stages of sleep.
