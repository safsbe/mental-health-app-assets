Adhering to good sleep hygiene is one way to achieve healthy sleep. Some sleep hygiene tips are:

  • Limit naps to 20mins
  • Engage in relaxing activities about 1hr prior to your bedtime to wind down
  • Ensure that your bedroom is cool, dark, quiet and peaceful
  • Avoid screen time as the light can stimulate your brain activities
  • Avoid alcohol, nicotine and caffeine prior to bedtime
  • Have at least 30mins of sunlight in the morning
Third, stick to the set sleep schedule continuously so your body can be conditioned to the right cues for sleep. It is normal if you find it hard to engage with sleep hygiene practices initially, as your body takes time to adjust, so keep it going.

Nonetheless if you have tried the above for a period and still find it ineffective, it is time to consult a doctor, especially if you suspect that it could be linked to an underlying medical/mental health condition.
