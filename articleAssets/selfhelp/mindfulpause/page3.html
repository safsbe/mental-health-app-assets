There are multiple benefits to mindful pauses such as:

Stress Reduction: Taking mindful pauses can help alleviate stress and anxiety, allowing us to re-evaluate where we are in our pursuit and approach challenges with a clearer mind.

Increased Productivity: Paradoxically, taking breaks can boost productivity. Mindful pauses provide an opportunity to recharge and rejuvenate, allowing us to return to our tasks with renewed focus and energy.
