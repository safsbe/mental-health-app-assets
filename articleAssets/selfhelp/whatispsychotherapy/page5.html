Which type of therapy should it be?

This depends on the needs and desired outcome. Far beyond the media portrayal of a person lying on a couch during therapy,  therapy sessions can be individual, couple, family, or even in group settings. There are also many techniques and approaches a mental health practitioner can utilise.  Each comes with its pros and cons.

Studies have found that a person took an average of 2.5 therapists to find a good match. So be patient.
