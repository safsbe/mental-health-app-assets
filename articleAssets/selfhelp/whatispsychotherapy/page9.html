Still lost on how to start? Try asking yourself these questions and share with your mental health provider to narrow down the options:

What type of concern do you want to address?

Is this concern recent or been there for a long time?

Do you want to focus on your diagnosed condition or everyday challenges?

Are you keener to understand the "why" or learn about "how" to change?

Remember there is no one way of help and the first step is to seek professional help as soon you can! :) Given the right treatment, these problems can be managed to help you go through your life smoothly and improve your overall mental well-being/ functioning in the future!
