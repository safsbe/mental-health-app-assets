SOME TYPES OF THERAPY

Therapy What is it Where can it be used for
Dialectical Behavioural Therapy (DBT) “Dialectical” refers to the situation where two opposites are true at the same time.

Balances acceptance and change by exploring contradictory or opposite perspective in different scenarios 		Good for borderline personality disorder or those with very strong opinions i.e. help them identify specific distressing behaviour and develop alternative perspectives to modify behaviour.
Humanistic Therapy Person-focused approach that aims at helping the person to reach full potential by becoming more self-aware and accepting of the thoughts, emotions and behaviour. Typically recommended for those with trauma, low self-esteem, mood issues or interpersonal difficulties
