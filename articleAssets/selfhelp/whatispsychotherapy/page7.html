SOME TYPES OF THERAPY

Therapy What is it Where can it be used for
Behavioural Therapy Suggests that new learning can fix a problem caused by previous learning

Uses conditioning and social learning to correct problematic behaviour or start a helpful behaviour 		Exposure therapy to overcome fears of situations/ objects/ people

Aversion therapy to pair an undesirable behaviour with undesirable stimulus/outcome to deter the unwanted behaviour
Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) Aims to identify unhelpful thought patterns and beliefs that causes problem.

Focuses on solving current challenges but does not go deep into past experiences 		Develop new problem-solving skills to overcome maladaptive coping strategies like addiction

Journalling and self-monitoring skills to manage anxiety or anger
